CLI-Rpg

1) Player is welcomed with...
	a) Message
	b) Number of players

2) Player is asked to battle against...
	a) Bots only
	b) Players and bots

3) Each turn in a battle, the player is asked to...
	a) Attack an enemy
		A) Decide weapon/spell
		B) Decide enemy
		C) Deal damage to enemy
	b) Run
		A) Player has to confirm
		B) If player has enough health, they can run away
		C) Otherwise they take damage
			Note) The more health they have, the harder they fall


Weapons:
- Sword
- Fireball
	- Burns
- Heal
- Bow
- Stun
- Rocket Launcher
- Regen Mana
